Uhoh

(On-Demand Professional Home Services)

My most recent contract project consisted of a Peer to Peer Home services app for customers and service providers. Uhoh consists of a customer app which facilitates service leads for Uhoh Pro service providers, which has its own provider app. The app uses Location Services, Google Firestore and Auth (for the backend), AVFoundation for the Camera and Photos, and some delegation and callbacks.

The UI was optimized for ease of use which sets this app apart from similar apps on the App Store.

Both apps are in the iOS App Store:

  • Uhoh!
  • Uhoh! Pro

Point Automotive

The Point Automotive App consisted of a Customer App as well as a Sales Person App.

This project aimed to simplify the car buying experience by removing the physical car dealership from the car buying process. Customers express interest in a vehicle and the Sales People make the sale happen.

Sales people could provide an up-to-the-minute lease price for customers.

This was a large project lasting a year and spanning four separate iOS and Web apps in total. We used API services from MarketScan (an automotive lease data provider)and Fuel (for stock images of all the cars). The app also incorporated VIN scanning & decoding for customers to monitor their vehicle health and mileage.

This app is currently on the iOS App Store:

  • Point Automotive (Customer App)

HighLoad

(Automated Diagnostics for macOS 10.4 and below)

HighLoad is a tool which will push your computer to the max to help diagnose most intermittent problems. This tool is meant for time testing a machine. The application will not diagnose the machine for you, you must gather info from the log files and develop a conclusion.

HighLoad can also run routine tasks such as rebuilding the Spotlight index files, and clearing System Cache files to make sure your computer is running at its best.

This application was published on Macupdate.com in 2005.
